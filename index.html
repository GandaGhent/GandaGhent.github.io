FAQ

Your questions, answered fast

Got a question about our themed city tours? Check below for quick answers or reach out to us anytime.

Which languages do guides speak?

Most tours are in English or Dutch. Some are also offered in French or Spanish. Let us know your choice when you book.

Are tours family-friendly?

Yes, most tours welcome kids! Check each tour for age tips or ask us if you’re unsure.

How long is a typical tour?

Tours last 1–2 hours. See each tour’s page for exact times.

Should I book ahead?

Booking early is best, especially in busy times. Walk-ins are possible if there’s space.

What happens if it rains?

Tours go on, rain or shine. Bring a raincoat or umbrella. We’ll contact you if weather forces changes.

Are tours accessible?

Many tours are accessible, but some routes have steps or cobbles. Contact us to find the best fit for you.
